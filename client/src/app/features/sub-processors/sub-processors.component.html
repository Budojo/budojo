What "sub-processor" means here

A sub-processor is any third party that, on Budojo's instruction, stores, transmits, or otherwise processes personal data of Budojo academy customers and their athletes — hosting providers, CDNs, mail-delivery vendors, error-tracking services.

A vendor that touches only Budojo's own corporate data (our source code on GitHub, our internal Slack, etc.) is not a sub-processor under this definition — academy customer data never reaches them.

Planned sub-processors (not yet active)

Each addition is preceded by a 30-day customer notification per the change process below. They are NOT active until that window has elapsed.

  • Email delivery vendor (TBD: Postmark / Resend / Mailgun EU) — transactional emails. Activates with M5 notifications. EU vendors preferred to avoid SCC overhead.
  • Sentry or comparable — browser and server error tracking with source-mapped stack traces. Activates if we adopt error tracking.
  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing for the Budojo SaaS subscription itself (NOT the academy ↔ athlete payment ledger, which lives in our DB). Activates at monetisation.

Change process

  1. Sub-processor change is opened as a PR updating this page and the source markdown.
  2. Every active academy customer is emailed at least 30 days before the new vendor goes live, with a link to this page and the activation date.
  3. Customers may object in writing within those 30 days. A blocking objection means we drop the change, or we terminate the customer's contract pro-rata.
  4. After the 30-day window with no blocking objections, the new vendor goes live.
